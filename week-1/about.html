Why am I taking this course?

When I was creating my final semester schedule, I was told I needed another cyber select credit. There were a few options for me, but the one that was most interesting was this course. I remember enjoying the web design course freshman year, so I figured it would be enjoyable to build on what I had learned at the beginning of my college career.

  • Improve HTML/CSS skills so I can build my own website
  • Get more comfortable using Github/Git
  • Be creative with my projects and experiment with design choices
